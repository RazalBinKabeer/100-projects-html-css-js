Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: Spaghetti, Eggs, Pancetta, Parmesan Cheese, Black Pepper

Instructions: Cook spaghetti. Fry pancetta. Mix eggs and cheese. Combine all with spaghetti.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients: Butter, Sugar, Eggs, Flour, Chocolate Chips, Baking Soda, Vanilla Extract

Instructions: Mix butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour, baking soda, and chocolate chips. Bake.
